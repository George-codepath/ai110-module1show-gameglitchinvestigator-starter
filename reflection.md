# 💭 Reflection: Game Glitch Investigator

Answer each question in 3 to 5 sentences. Be specific and honest about what actually happened while you worked. This is about your process, not trying to sound perfect.

## 1. What was broken when you started?

- What did the game look like the first time you ran it?
- List at least two concrete bugs you noticed at the start  
  (for example: "the secret number kept changing" or "the hints were backwards").

  At the start, I noticed that the New Game button did not fully reset the game. It changed the secret number and attempts, but it left status as "won" or "lost", so the app could still block play right after restarting. I also saw that the UI always said “Guess a number between 1 and 100,” even when the selected difficulty used a different range. Another clear bug was that New Game always picked a secret from 1 to 100, which ignored the difficulty setting.
